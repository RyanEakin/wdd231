Site Name: Museum of Web Dev

Purpose: this Museum is to showcase web devlopment Concepts I was taught throughout the Web Programming Certificate course from BYU-pathway. where it dynamically allows for the site to be referenced as a form of living documentation so that I may reference it back when developing websites and other web-based technologies

Scenerios:

  • why make the game and the documentation seperately? most people try to have their documentation separate from the work itself. this causes issues with keeping the content updated in a timely fashion and increases overhead. by having the documentaion being a PART of the project we can side-step that issue and utilize references to make documentation AND the porject at the same time
  • how best to keep web dev knowledge without having to take out a small loan? most people who work through Web Development or some other course attempt to do things by themselves alone after learning the course to sometimes find that they are floundering on one point or another causing the issue of having to dive back into the depths of the internet or spend another large sum of money for even more education. by creating this site I will side-step that issue by looking through my own notes and making them available for both me AND others to peruse and remind them about how to do front-end web development [base version]
